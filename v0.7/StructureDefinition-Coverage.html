Resource Profile: HCX Coverage

Defining URL:http://hl7.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/HCXCoverage
Version:0.0.1
Name:Coverage
Status:Draft as of 11/1/19 9:29 AM (Standards Status: Trial Use)
Definition:

Financial instrument which may be used to reimburse or pay for health care products and services. Includes both insurance and self-payment.

Publisher:HL7 International - HCX
Committee:Financial Management
Maturity:2
